# ReviewPost — Phase 4 Distribution Plan

> Launch channels, SEO strategy, white-label agency program, and partnership integrations.
> Distribution moat > feature moat. Win on relationships first.

---

## Launch Channels

### ProductHunt

**Timing:** Launch after reaching 50 stable users (validates the product).

**Preparation:**
- [ ] Create a compelling GIF/video demo (30 sec max)
- [ ] Write a clear tagline: "Turn your Google reviews into social posts. Automatically."
- [ ] Prepare 5 example cards from different business niches
- [ ] Line up 10–15 supporters to upvote on launch day
- [ ] Schedule for Tuesday–Thursday, 12:01 AM PST

**Goal:** Top 5 of the day → 500+ new signups.

---

### AppSumo (Lifetime Deal)

**Timing:** Month 8–10. Use to get a burst of users + cash.

**Deal structure:**
- $49 one-time → Solo plan lifetime access (capped at 1,000 codes)
- $99 one-time → Business plan lifetime access (capped at 500 codes)

**Revenue estimate:** 500 codes × $49 = $24,500 one-time.
**Risk:** Lifetime users generate support without recurring revenue. Cap codes strictly.

**How to get listed:**
1. Apply at [appsumo.com/submit](https://appsumo.com/submit)
2. AppSumo takes 70% of revenue (keep this in mind for pricing)

## SEO Strategy

### Target Keywords

**Primary (high intent):**
- "google reviews social media posts"
- "auto post reviews instagram"
- "turn google reviews into social posts"
- "review to post generator"

**Niche-specific (lower competition):**
- "dental office social media posts"
- "salon instagram post ideas reviews"
- "HVAC company social media content"

### Content Strategy

Create one landing page per niche:
```
/for/salons          → "ReviewPost for Salons & Barbershops"
/for/dentists        → "ReviewPost for Dental Clinics"
/for/gyms            → "ReviewPost for Gyms & Personal Trainers"
/for/home-services   → "ReviewPost for HVAC, Plumbers & Electricians"
/for/real-estate     → "ReviewPost for Real Estate Agents"
```

Each page:
- Shows 3 example cards from that niche
- Has niche-specific copy
- CTA → demo tool with the niche pre-selected

**Blog:** Target "how to get more customers from Google reviews" type keywords.
One article per week. AI-assisted drafts, human-edited.

## White-Label Agency Program

### The Pitch

> "Resell ReviewPost under your brand. Charge $200–400/mo per client.
> You pay $149/mo flat — unlimited clients, unlimited posts."

**Agency plan features:**
- Custom logo + brand colors (your agency's brand, not ReviewPost)
- Custom domain (e.g. `reviews.youragency.com`)
- Manage multiple client accounts from one dashboard
- Team access (add account managers)
- No ReviewPost watermark or branding anywhere

### How to Reach Agencies

1. **Cold outreach to social media agencies** — find on LinkedIn, filter by "local business" clients
2. **Facebook groups** — "Social Media Marketing", "Agency Owners", "SMMA"
3. **Upwork/Fiverr agencies** — offer as a value-add tool

### Economics

At $149/mo per agency with 10 clients at $250/mo each:
- Agency earns: $2,500/mo − $149 = $2,351/mo margin
- ReviewPost earns: $149/mo × 10 agencies = $1,490/mo (no support per client)

## Partnerships

### POS / Booking Tools

Integrate with tools local businesses already use — position ReviewPost as an add-on:

| Tool | Business Type | Integration Type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Square | Retail, food, services | OAuth + review trigger on payment |
| Mindbody | Fitness, wellness, salons | OAuth + review trigger post-visit |
| Vagaro | Salons, spas | Partner app listing |
| Housecall Pro | Home services (HVAC, plumbers) | Partner app listing |

**Approach:** Start with partner app listings (no code). Deep integrations come after 100+ mutual customers.
