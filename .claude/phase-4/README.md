# ReviewPost — Phase 4: Growth

> Month 6–12. Goal: scale beyond Google + IG/FB, add AI upgrades, build distribution.
> Target: 300 paying users + 5 agency accounts = $10,000+ MRR.

---

## Overview

Phase 4 assumes Phase 3 Product v1 is live and stable.
Growth focuses on three levers:

1. **More review sources** — Yelp, Trustpilot, TripAdvisor, App Store
2. **More social platforms** — LinkedIn, TikTok, Google Business posts
3. **AI upgrades** — smarter selection, optimal posting times, hashtags
4. **Distribution** — ProductHunt, AppSumo, white-label agencies, SEO, partnerships

## Prioritization

### High Impact (build first)
1. Yelp reviews (large source, widely used by local businesses)
2. LinkedIn publishing (B2B service providers)
3. Hashtag generation (low effort, high perceived value)
4. White-label for agencies (Agency plan upsell)

### Medium Impact
5. Trustpilot integration (official API available)
6. Optimal posting time AI
7. ProductHunt + AppSumo launch
8. SEO content strategy

### Lower Priority / Later
9. TikTok video cards
10. Google Business posts
11. App Store / Google Play reviews
12. G2, Capterra listings
